# Knowledge Model Glossary

## Observation

An objective record of reality captured at a specific time.

Example:

Cow 744 temperature = 39.4°C.

## Information

Structured and contextualized observation data.

Example:

Cow 744 temperature is 1.2°C above normal range.

## Knowledge

A meaningful interpretation derived from multiple validated observations and context.

Example:

Cow 744 may be showing signs of health deterioration.

## Recommendation

An explainable suggested action generated by FarmOS to support human decision-making.

## Decision

An action selected by an authorized human in response to a recommendation or operational need.

## Action

Work performed after a decision.

Example:

Schedule vet visit, isolate animal, change feed ration.

## Outcome

The measured result of an action.

Example:

Milk recovered, diagnosis confirmed, recommendation rejected.

## Learning

The process by which outcomes improve future recommendations.

## Evidence

The observations, records, history, and context supporting a recommendation.

## Confidence

A score representing how strongly the available evidence supports a knowledge object or recommendation.

## Missing Observation

An expected observation that was not recorded.

Example:

No milk entry recorded for a lactating cow.

## Validation

The process of checking whether an observation is technically complete, biologically plausible, and business-valid.

## Digital Twin

The authoritative digital representation of a real farm entity.

## Institutional Memory

The long-term knowledge base of the farm, preserved independently of individual employees.
